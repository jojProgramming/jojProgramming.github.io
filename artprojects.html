Design & Photographic Showcase

by JOJProgramming

Capturing the best of the UK, small...

For over three years I have been trying to capture some of the best bits of the UK. This includes (but, I do ever so hope is not limited to) birds, bugs, and bridges.

...and big.

Not only has this been a great hobby of mine, but I've also learnt about interface design through the exploration of image editors like DarkTable, Photoshop, and more.

Game Assets

I've also spent some time getting the hang of LibreSprite, a Free and Open-Source fork of Asperite. During this time I've not only produced assets to use while I explore the Godot game engine, but also assets such as that pictured left for use in my peers' games.

Iconography

While its often best practice to use a third party icon set, I've also enjoyed learning inkscape, a free and open source vector graphics editor.

Art should be accessible to everyone. That's why whenever I can, I try to use and promote Free and Open-Source software.
